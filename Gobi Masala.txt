1. Heat oil
2. add ginger and garlic and chilli
3. Put Onion, caramalize
4. Add Potato, Cauliflower(gobi) and peas ( carrots, optionally.)
5. Add spices - Garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric, chilli powder, salt, sabji masasla (optional)
5. cover with a plate, put a lid for 10 -15 mins on medium heat
6. Open add diced tomato ( 4-6 pieces)
7. Cook for 5 mins
8. Garnsih with coriander and serve hot.
